Risotto alla milanese: A delicious meal!

Description

Typical Italian risotto as the tradition of my city wants it. Directly from Milano I send you this wonderful recipe!

Nutrition Facts: 834 Calories, 35g fats, 90 carbs and 28 proteins.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4cups uncooked Arborio rice
  • 1/2cup unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 1/2 quarts beef stock
  • 3 tablespoons beef marrow
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon saffron powder
  • 3/4 cup dry white wine
  • salt to taste
  • 1 1/2cups grated Parmesan cheese
